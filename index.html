Providential Learning

The platform for learning & skills transfer

How it Works

  • Learn at your own pace
  • Get full access to courses available and boost your skills
  • Be part of the training and submit your courses divided in modules
  • As a student discuss with other students via online group sessions
  • Get monthly newsletters related to your school or any educational programs near you
  • Fully personalise the user interface to suit your needs
  • Fitness coaches can upload their training sessions for students to exercise
  • Career guidance available
  • Nutrition guide available
  • Providential Learning cares about mental health. Get full access to mental health guides
  • All for R99.00/month per student

About

Providential Learning offers services to teachers and students for online learning

It is made for both urban dwellers and those in remote places in mind

Tutors can assist teachers by interacting with students in group chats

Not only will it be about the traditional school curriculum but skill sharing is also part of the platform

The objective is to equip young minds with skills that will cater the Fourth Industrial Revolution
